About Us

expand_circle_down

Founded in 2015 by two enthusiasts for aquatic plants our company is currently producing high quality water plants for use in fish tanks, ponds, zoos and many more locations.
We focus on plants growing completetly under the surface (submers as in submersible), as well as plants that grow "with wet feet", i.e. roots and rhizoma are under water with leaves and flowers growing on or above surface level (emers).
Our mission is to provide a wide variety of high quality, healthy plants, that help others shape their water eco-systems to their needs and liking.
Our special focus lies on fast growing, completely clean plants, able to be introduced in the highest possible variety of settings.
With clean plants, we mean to provide the advertised organisms only, so no "surprise visitors" (such as snails, fungi parts of other plants) can enter our customer eco-systems via our plants.
Of course all of our plants are free of pesticide, fungicides or all other types of potential poisonous substances and are delivered within minimal time to ensure maximum potential for growth.
